Moveset: Clodsire is a bulky Pokemon with low speed, so it's best used as a early game settup.

 

Clodsire @ Black Sludge

Ability: Water Absorb

EVs: 252 HP / 24 Def / 232 SpD

Careful Nature

- Earthquake

- Toxic Spikes

- Toxic

- Recover

 

So what you do is just use toxic spikes normally first turn (use once for poison, use twice for toxic poison.)

Toxic for if you want to give them a hard time and to chunck out their HP during turns, Earthquake for STAB, and Recover to heal. Remember, your speed is dead low, so use your moves accordingly and wisely.
